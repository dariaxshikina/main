StayColdGoods

Shipping pre-made items

We process and send orders from Monday to Friday.
All shipments are managed through USPS and will be shipped within 48 hours after receiving payment.
Estimated shipping times:

Deliveries in the USA: 3-5 business days.
Deliveries to other countries: 2-4+ weeks, depending on customs procedures.

Shipping custom items

All custom items are made especially for you after the order is placed and payment is received.
Estimated process times: up to 14 days

Tracking an order

As soon as we have sent your order, you will receive an email informing you of the tracking number and the link to track your package.

Return policy

Products may be returned within 5 days of receipt for a store credit less the original shipping cost. Customers are responsible for any return shipping costs. Used or final sale items cannot be returned.
Once we receive the returned item(s), we will credit your original method of payment excluding non-refundable shipping costs as soon as possible. Please note refunds may take 2-3 weeks to process and show on your account.

How to return an item

Make sure that your order qualifies and inform us via contact form. Unused products may be returned within 5 days of receipt.
