Hi! I'm glad you made it to this page.
Thank you for your interest in my small business.

StayColdGoods is the brand with only one person behind it. My name is Daria, and I learned how to crochet as a little girl. I used to make doilies and potholders for fun. As I grew older, I found different hobbies and forgot about crochet.

When 2020 and COVID happened, I'd got too much free time on my hands. Here I remembered about crochet.
It turned out it's also a great way to reduce anxiety, so I've been crocheting non-stop ever since.
At some point, I ended up with too many goods, so I started to sell them, and here we are.
