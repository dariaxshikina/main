Where are you based and do you ship worldwide?

We are based in the US and we ship worldwide

How long does shipping take?

All of the pre made items are ready to ship within 1-3 days. Any custom items will take up to 14 days to be made and shipped. Everything ships out first class USPS. Please note international orders usually take 2-4+ weeks to arrive.

Do you make custom tops?

Yes. Please use order form to place your order.

What if my package is lost?

Unfortunately, the post office loses packages or items can get mis-delivered. If your package is lost, please contact your local post office to open a claim. We are not responsible for lost or damaged packages, but we can find a solution together to work it out so feel free to contact us!

What if the item doesn't fit?

Our sizing is very detailed and the tops themselves can be very adjustable. Please make sure to double check the size-chart and your measurements to insure that the top you are ordering fits. If you happen to measure inaccurately, not read our size-chart, or not double check the size of the item you ordered, you can return the item for store credit minus flat return shipping fee of $5.

How do i care for my crochet goods?

Gently hand wash in cold water using mild detergent and lay flat to air dry. I do NOT recommend machine washing any handmade clothing items. If you must, please make sure you put the item(s) in a mesh laundry bag to prevent any snagging. Do NOT machine dry, as the item may shrink. These items are delicate so please keep that in mind. With right care crochet items will last very long time!

What material do you use with your crochet goods?

Most of our goods are made with 100% cotton. But sometimes we use acrylic or polyester. Materials of each item are listed in the item's description.
