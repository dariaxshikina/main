Cup width is the measurement of the widest part of the cup.

Overbust measurement paired with your bra measurement can be beneficial in determining the correct fitting bralette top.

One bralette top can accommodate multiple bra sizes.

Most of the tops are made with an adjustable corset-style back design that you can tighten or loosen as you like.

If you cant find your bra size in this size-chart you should search for your sister size.
